# Recommendations

Use this section to present actionable next steps based on the findings and limitations of the study.

## Required Coverage

Recommendations may include:

- improvements for the next system version
- additional validation or deployment work
- technical refinements for the core logic or architecture
- broader rollout, integration, or scaling considerations
- future research directions that remain outside the present MVP

At minimum, the final manuscript should provide two to three concrete recommendations that follow directly from the reported findings or limitations.

## Recommendation Quality Check

Each recommendation should be:

- specific enough that a future team can act on it
- connected to a reported limitation, unmet target, or improvement opportunity
- realistic within the project's domain and technical context

## Writing Reminder

Recommendations should follow directly from the findings and limitations. Avoid adding unrelated ideas that were never motivated by the results.
