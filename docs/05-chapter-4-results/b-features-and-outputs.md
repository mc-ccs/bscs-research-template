# Features and Outputs

Use this section to present the implemented features, major outputs, and concrete evidence that the approved MVP was delivered.

This section is a recurring sprint reporting area under **Epic 4 - User Story 2**. Keep the narrative aligned with the per-sprint appendix evidence so completed outputs and deferred scope remain traceable.

## Required Coverage

For each major feature or module, report:

- the requirement, story, or objective addressed
- what was implemented
- the output produced by the system
- the evidence used to verify the feature
- whether the implemented output satisfied its acceptance criteria or expected behavior
- any remaining limitation relevant to interpretation

## Figures and Screenshots

Use figures only when they provide clear evidence such as:

- interface screenshots of major workflows
- output reports or generated results
- architecture or logic artifacts referenced in the implementation discussion

## Writing Reminder

Do not repeat the full methodology here. Focus on what was actually built and what the artifact produced.
