## How to Use the Matrix

After completing the table, summarize the recurring patterns you found, such as:

- repeated limitations in existing systems
- methods that perform well but are costly or difficult to deploy
- workflow gaps that remain unaddressed in the local context
- evaluation methods commonly used in related work
