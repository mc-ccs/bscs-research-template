# Scope and Limitations

Use this section to define the MVP boundary clearly so reviewers can distinguish what the team committed to deliver from what is intentionally excluded.

## Recommended Coverage

State the following:

- the features included in the MVP
- the intended users, deployment setting, or coverage boundary
- major technical, operational, or data-related constraints
- features, integrations, or conditions that are explicitly out of scope

## Suggested Structure

### 1. MVP Scope

List the essential functions that must be present for the system to demonstrate the proposed solution.

### 2. Operational Boundaries

State where and for whom the system applies, such as one campus, one office, one user group, or one device class.

### 3. Limitations

Document realistic constraints such as unavailable integrations, limited training data, single-platform support, small respondent count, or prototype-only deployment.
